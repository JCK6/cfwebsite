ABOUT THE GAME:

Crossfire is an online tactical first-person shooter game developed by Smilegate Entertainment for Microsoft Windows. It was first released in South Korea on May 3, 2007.

The world has changed drastically since the dawn of the new millenium. Nations and organizations have started walking down the road of self-perservation with the impending signs of resource scardity and global warming while anarchists and terrorists are able to run wild in areas outside life interest of the world's super powers. Disgruntled with this "new world order" many soldiers from the world's leading special forces founded the mercenary corporation: "Global Risk". This organization stands to uphold peace and combat terrorism around the world to prevent the world from falling into chao. The only wars they participate in are those with jistifitable and moral causes, regardless of how much they gget paid; almost as to atone for the atrocities they have commited while serving the world's super powers. The Global Risk Corporation's main clients are developing countries that don't have have the regular armies where terrorism runs apart.

It is believed that the emergence of the Global Risk Corporation was the result of the activities of secret organization only known as the "Black List". They have claimed to be responsible for
the most atrocious acts of terrorism this century. An organization driven driven by profit, they will take on any mission as long as the price is right.
